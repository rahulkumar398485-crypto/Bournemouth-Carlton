Vacation with Your Dog

The Bournemouth Carlton Hotel is a pet-friendly Victorian hotel located on the East Cliff, offering a welcoming environment for guests traveling with dogs. Pets of any size are welcome, making it easy to enjoy your stay with your four-legged companion.

Dogs are welcome in selected rooms, including spacious accommodations with sea views and private balconies. A fee of £25 per pet per night applies, and guests are kindly requested to inform the hotel in advance. Food and water bowls are provided, and dogs are allowed in designated lounge and bar areas, as well as outdoor garden spaces.
