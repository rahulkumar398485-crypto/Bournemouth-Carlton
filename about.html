Our Story

A Historic Seafront Landmark

A Legacy Since 1903

The Bournemouth Carlton Hotel is a historic Victorian-era hotel located on the East Cliff, known for its elegant architecture, dramatic bay views and rich heritage. It has welcomed royalty, celebrities and world leaders throughout its history.

A Place in History

During World War II, General Eisenhower and Field Marshal Montgomery met here to discuss D-Day plans. Today, the hotel combines timeless charm with modern comfort for unforgettable stays.
